Travel Tips

Pack Light.

As a People's Republic, many services are provided free of cost. That includes healthcare, transport, and public hotels. We allow you to focus on attaining your goals without having to pay 35 dinarii a night.

Be Sensitive.

The Republic has a strong socialist heritage. We hope that visitors respect the culture of our citizens while here.

Have Fun!

There is so much to explore here in Rome. Keep an open mind: you never know what amazing things you will see.
