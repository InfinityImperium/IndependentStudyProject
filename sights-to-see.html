Sights to See!

Hall of Fallen Workers

Location: Roma

Built in prid. Non. Mart. anno MMDCXL A.U.C., the Hall of Fallen Workers is a testament to all who have died in service of the liberation of the masses from the Revolution till the present time.

It is recommended for visitors to pay their respects to these brave martyrs.

The Colosseum

Location: Roma

Originally built for gladiatorial contests and dramas, the Colosseum now serves as the entertainment hub of the Republic. Artists from all across Roma come here to share their art to an adoring crowd. Tickets cost 37 denarii each.

Church of the Holy Sepulchre

Location: Judaea

The Church of the Holy Sepulchre is the location where Christ was crucified and resurrected. Furthermore, it was the location of the famous Grand Address by the First Commissioner of the RPR Aries Calvus, who declared Christianity and Popularism as not only compatible, but as natural partners. The Church thus represents a crucial cornerstone of Roman identity.
